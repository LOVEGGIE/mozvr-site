Getting Started

  • iPhone
  • Android
  • Oculus Rift
  • Other

To use your iPhone as a virtual reality device.

      Load a WebVR scene in your favorite browser
      Hold up your phone and move it around to "look"
      Tap the VR button and drop it into a a Cardboard holder.
      You've got WebVR on your iPhone!

To use your Android phone as a virtual reality device.

      Load a WebVR scene in your favorite browser
      Hold up your phone and move it around to "look"
      Tap the VR button and drop it into a a Cardboard holder.
      You've got WebVR on your phone!
