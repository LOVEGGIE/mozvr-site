Getting Started

  • iPhone
  • Android
  • Oculus Rift
  • Other

To use your iPhone as a virtual reality device.

  1. Load a WebVR scene in your favorite browser
  2. Hold up your phone and move it around to "look"
  3. Tap the VR button and drop it into a a Cardboard holder. You've got WebVR on your iPhone!

To use your Android phone as a virtual reality device.

  1. Load a WebVR scene in your favorite browser
  2. Hold up your phone and move it around to "look"
  3. Tap the VR button and drop it into a a Cardboard holder.
    4. You've got WebVR on your phone!

Developers

WebVR provides support for exposing virtual reality devices like the Oculus Rift to web apps, enabling developers to create virtual reality experiences that run on any WebGL-enabled brower.
